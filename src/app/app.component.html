How can I transfer money from Paytm wallet to bank account? keyboard_arrow_down
  • Transfer money to your bank anytime, anywhere!
  • Open the Paytm App & tap on. Passbook. Select 'Send Money to bank' Tap on 'Transfer' to proceed.
  • Enter details (Amount, Account. Number, IFSC) & click 'Send' to. complete the transaction.
  • It's done! Money has been. transferred successfully.
Can we send money from Paytm to bank account? keyboard_arrow_down

If you are a new Paytm user without KYC, you have to wait three days to transfer money from your Paytm Wallet to bank account. ... You can send a minimum of Rs. 100 to your bank account via the service. Every Paytm users can transfer up to Rs. 5,000 at a time, with 25,000 per month the limit.
